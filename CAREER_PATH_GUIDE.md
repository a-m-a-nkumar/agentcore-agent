## 💼 Specific Job Titles You Will Qualify For

### 1. **AI Engineer / AI Application Engineer**
*   **What they do:** specialized software engineers who build applications *using* LLMs. They don't train models (usually); they orchestrate them.
*   **Why you fit:** You know how to manage `AgentCore` logic, handle long-running LLM requests (AsyncIO), and store conversation history (PostgreSQL/Vector DBs).
*   **Average US Salary Range:** $140k - $220k+

### 2. **Senior Python Backend Engineer**
*   **What they do:** Build the heavy-lifting logic for servers.
*   **Why you fit:** By mastering **FastAPI, Pydantic, and Connection Pooling** (Week 1-4), you prove you aren't just a "scripter" but a software *architect* who cares about performance and type safety.
*   **Average US Salary Range:** $130k - $190k

### 3. **Cloud Solutions Architect (AWS)**
*   **What they do:** Design secure and scalable systems on the cloud.
*   **Why you fit:** By learning **S3 Events, Lambda Triggers, and IAM Roles** (Week 5-8), you show you understand "Serverless" architecture, which is the modern standard for cost-effective scaling.
*   **Average US Salary Range:** $150k - $210k
